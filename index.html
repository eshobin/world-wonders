Additional Information

The Wonders of the World are a selection of places, both natural and man-made, recognized for their remarkable beauty, historical significance, or engineering feats. There are two primary lists: the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World and the New Seven Wonders of the World. The ancient list was compiled centuries ago, while the new list was chosen through a global vote in 2007.
